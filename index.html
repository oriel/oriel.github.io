Oriel Frigo

Hi, welcome!

I am currently a PhD student on “Example guided video editing”, within a thesis agreement between Technicolor and Université Paris Descartes.

In July 2013, I completed the Master program "Color in Informatics and Media Technology" (CIMET). During this period, I have studied at Université Jean Monnet and University of Eastern Finland. My master thesis internship took place at Technicolor, on the topic of “Example guided color transfer”, under supervision of Pierre Hellier and Neus Sabater.

In 2012 I received the BSc degree in Computer Science from Universidade do Estado de Santa Catarina (Brazil), with a dissertation entitled " Automatic Classiﬁcation of Engravings Based on Texture Analysis and Self-organizing Maps ".

My research interests include computer vision, color science and pattern recognition.
